Hey! I am a second-year PhD student in Multi-Robot Systems Group (MRS) at the Czech Technical University advised by Dr. Robert Pěnička and Prof. Martin Saska. My research focuses on using learning-based methods for generalisable, multi-goal, and multi-task agile UAV flight. In the long term, I aim to develop a unified controller that can operate safely at high speeds in cluttered, real-world outdoor environments. >
Before my PhD, I worked on classical nonlinear control strategies for UAVs carrying different types of payloads, such as suspended loads and aerial manipulators at RRC, IIIT-H advised by Dr. Spandan Roy ,  Prof. Simone Baldi and Dr. Jinoh Lee.

At MRS, I have the opportunity to work with an exceptional team that regularly deploys UAVs in outdoor, real-world conditions. Here is a glimpse into what our group does.
