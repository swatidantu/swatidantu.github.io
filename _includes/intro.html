Hey! I am a second year PhD student at Multi-Robot Systems Group (MRS) in Czech Technical University advised by Dr. Robert Penicka and Prof. Martin Saska. My current research interests revolve around leveraging the power of learning based methods for generalisable, multi-goal and multi-task agile flight. Long term, I would like to arrive at a unified controller able to operate at super high speeds in cluttered environments in outdoor, real world scenarios!

Prior to this I worked on classical non-linear control methods for UAV flight aimed at a carrying a payload via different mechanisms such as suspended payload and an aerial manipulator at RRC, IIIT-H advised by Dr. Spandan Roy,  Prof. Simone Baldi and Dr. Jinoh Lee.
